Description:
------------
This bot farms Alpine Seeds. The droprate is really low, feel free to make a more efficient one, e.g. in Northern Shiverpeaks.

Requirements:
-------------
- Outpost: Copperhammer Mines (Prophecies)
- Decent hero team

Feel free to do a better build in your own. I can't motivate myself to think of proper builds every single week for these kind of scripts :).

Keep in mind this is a quick, dirty and not very efficient script. However, it will be okay to satisfy your own needs.
